# Gordon Ramsay Chocolate Mousse

## Ingredients

* 55g eggs (3 eggs)
* 125g dark cooking chocolate (70% cocoa)
* 10g unsalted butter
* 120ml full-fat cream
* 3 tablespoons caster sugar (superfine sugar) (granulated works)
Optional: 
* More whipped cream for serving
* Chocolate shavings for garnish

## Steps

* Separate egg whites and yolks
* * Whites in  bowl
* * Yellows in small dish - hand whisk to smooth, no lumps
* * Use room temperature eggs for better volume.

* Melt chocolate and butter in microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring until smooth.
* * Cool slightly. (ice or water bath)
* * Don’t overheat; chocolate can seize.

* Measure cream into mixing bowl.
* * Whip cream to stiff peaks.	Chill your bowl for better results.

* Add sugar to egg whites and whip to firm, glossy peaks.	Use a clean bowl to avoid any grease.
* Fold egg yolks into whipped cream.
* Add melted chocolate and fold again.	Be gentle to keep the air in.
* Gently fold in whipped egg whites in batches.	Use a spatula for a light touch.

* Spoon into serving glasses and chill at least 6 hours.	Overnight chilling is even better!
* Serve topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
